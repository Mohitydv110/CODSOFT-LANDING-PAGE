coca-cola, or coke, is a carbonated soft drink manufactures by the coca cola company. In 1888 pemberton sold coca cola ownership rights to Asa Griggs Candler, a businessman, whose marketing tactics led coca cola to its dominance of the global soft drink marketthroughout the 20th and 21th century. The drink name refers to two of its original ingredients: coca leavesand kola nuts( a source ofcaggeine). The drink has inspired imitators and created a whole classification of soft drink:colas.
